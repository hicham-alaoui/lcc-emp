Welcome to the Leeds City College Substantial Industry Placements Information Hub

The clue is in the name - SIPs are SUBSTANTIAL which means they differ from traditional work placements in that they:

  • Offer a longer placement period which allows for a more meaningful experience.
  • Are specifically aligned to your studies and career aspirations.
  • Provide 1:1 support from your Career Mentor before, during and after your placement.
  • Give you access to valuable and fun, interactive workshops to prepare you for the working world!
  • Provide a true insight of what it is like to work in your chosen sector and gain knowledge, skills and information direct from the source (people who work in industry).

Benefits

  • Develop technical skills and industry knowledge which will increase their industry competence and in turn assist with future employment.
  • Gain an in-depth industry relevant reference that will help in applying for future job roles.
  • Gain a deeper knowledge of the everyday running of successful business
  • Enhance all areas of employability skills such as communication, team work, organisation and planning.
